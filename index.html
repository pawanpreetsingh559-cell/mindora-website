How do I unlock a butterfly?
Mindora follows a unique 4-session lifecycle. You start as an Egg, progress to a Caterpillar, then a Pupa. After completing your 4th micro-reset session, you reach the Butterfly stage and permanently add a new butterfly to your AR Garden!
Do I have to do long meditations?
Not at all. Mindora was built specifically for people who don't have time for 30-minute sessions. Our core toolkit focuses entirely on 2-minute micro-resets. We also offer 7 Advanced Calming exercises for deeper sessions when you want them.
Is my personal mood data private?
Absolutely. Your analytics, emotional trends, and profiles are encrypted securely via Supabase. We never sell data to third parties, health trackers, or ad brokers. You can delete all your data permanently from the Profile tab at any time.
Will my exercise stop if someone calls me?
Yes, gracefully. Mindora features Smart Session Pausing. If you receive a phone call, your breathing exercise or calming sound seamlessly pauses and waits for you to resume when you're ready.
